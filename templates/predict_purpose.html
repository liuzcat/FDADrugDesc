This is a prototype classification model (~83% validation accuracy) with 64 FDA-approved drug purpose categories. These were drawn from the dataset, as long as these specific categories had over 200 drugs attributed to them. This is certainly not a complete picture of all FDA-approved drugs or even FDA-approved drugs with labeled purposes.

Type in comma-separated active and/or inactive ingredients of your drug to see what purpose it gets sorted into.
